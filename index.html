Our Products

 

Reverse Osmosis Desalination

  • Treating highly saline water
  • Cater to a wide range of capacities
  • Custom-build systems

Water RO technology is utilized for treating highly saline water with a salinity level of up to 50,000 ppm, ensuring the production of clean and safe drinking water.

 

Thermal Forward Osmosis Desalination

  • Innovative TFO technology
  • Remarkable energy savings achieved
  • Significant cost advantages

We employ the innovative TFO technology specifically designed for irrigation purposes. TFO takes advantage of renewable energy sources like solar thermal, geothermal, or industrial waste heat to power the water treatment process.

 

Demineralisation System

  • Reverse osmosis system
  • Eliminating the need for chemicals
  • Flexible Implementation

Complementing our reverse osmosis system, our ion-exchange and EDI demineralization units enhance ultrapure water production. Our EDI system demineralizes water using electricity and ion exchange membranes, eliminating the need for chemicals.

 

Industrial Waste Water Treatment

  • Chemical Waste Reactor (CWR)
  • Membrane Bio-Reactor (MBR)
  • Anaerobic Biological Reactor (ABR)

Our CWR effectively treats a wide range of contaminants such as toxic organics, inorganics, and heavy metals. The MBR combines biological treatment with submerged ultra or microfiltration membranes, eliminating the need for sedimentation tanks As a pre-treatment option for high BOD waste water, our ABR provides unique advantages.
